# Sylph — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Sylph — connotes rarity, grace, and quiet prestige
- **Positioning**: The world's most tasteful luxury watch and wallet boutique, in your pocket
- **Audience**: Professional athletes, musicians, Hollywood millionaires — image-conscious, affluent men

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Near-black / deep slate (#0D0D0F, #111114) — anchors the editorial darkness
- **Surface**: Dark charcoal (#1A1A1E, #1E1E22) — cards, panels, modals
- **Accent / Gold**: Warm champagne gold (#C9A96E, #B8966A) — CTAs, highlights, price tags, icons
- **Text Primary**: Off-white (#F5F5F0) — headlines, primary copy
- **Text Secondary**: Muted warm grey (#9B9B8F) — metadata, captions, labels
- **Dividers / Borders**: #2A2A2E — subtle separators
- **Destructive / Alert**: #8B3A3A — reserved, never overused

## Typography
- **Display / Headlines**: Serif typeface (Playfair Display or similar) — editorial authority, brand stories, hero text
- **Body / UI**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar) — readable, modern, never decorative
- **Sizing**: Generous leading, ample whitespace — luxury signals restraint, not density
- **Weight contrast**: Heavy serif headlines against light sans-serif body for visual hierarchy

## Elevation & Depth
- Dark surfaces with very subtle inner glow or shimmer borders (gold at ~10–15% opacity) on featured cards
- No heavy drop shadows — use layered dark backgrounds to create depth
- Hero product imagery: full-bleed or near-full-bleed, always high-resolution

## Component Style
- **Cards**: Dark background, borderless or hairline gold border on featured items; rounded corners (8–12px)
- **Buttons (Primary)**: Deep gold fill (#C9A96E), near-black text, medium weight — never screaming
- **Buttons (Secondary)**: Transparent with gold hairline border, gold text
- **Input Fields**: Dark surface, subtle gold focus ring, understated placeholder text
- **Navigation**: Minimal bottom tab bar, icon-first, no labels unless selected
- **Badges / Tags**: Muted, small-cap sans-serif — "Certified", "Limited Edition", "New Arrival"

## Imagery & Media
- Photography: Editorial-grade product shots — dramatic lighting, dark backgrounds, macro detail
- Never stock-photo generic; every image should feel like a magazine spread
- Brand editorial sections: full-width hero images with overlay text, cinematic crop

## Interaction & Motion
- Transitions: Smooth, unhurried — 250–350ms ease-in-out
- No bouncy or playful animations — gravity and restraint
- Scroll-driven reveals: content fades in as user scrolls, never jumps

## Layout Principles
- Mobile-first; designed for thumb navigation
- Ample whitespace — content breathes, never cluttered
- Grid: 16px base margin, 8px spacing unit
- Hero sections full-width; catalog in 2-column grid on mobile
- Editorial pages: single-column, magazine-width, generous vertical rhythm

## Apple Human Interface Guidelines Alignment
- Respect safe areas and dynamic type scaling
- Haptic feedback on key actions (inquiry submitted, item saved)
- Bottom sheets for quick actions, full screens for editorial depth
- Tab bar with 4–5 items maximum; icons refined and minimal
